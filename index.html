~ One Who Brings The Death..

He is the one who Controls the Dead. He himself is Death. When he walks Hundreds of Thousands of Deads follow him. Those who end up being in his way become One of those following him on his way to the End. There is No One who can withstand his Emmense Power and Arya Stark is that No One...


~ Who Is He?

Some call him The Death , Some call him The End , But the Legends Refer to him as "The Night King."


~ What Things Can He Do?

  • He can kill anyone with just one Touch of his sword.
  • He can raise the White Walkers who are known as the sons of The Night King.
  • He can bring Freezing Winter Storms that kills every life form.
  • He can raise the Dead to join his Army.

~ Who Can Kill Him??

There are some things one should know before confronting The Death Himself!!

  1. He cannot be killed with Fire.
  2. He cannot be killed with any normal Sword.
  3. He can be killed only by a Valyrian Steel Sword right into the Heart.
